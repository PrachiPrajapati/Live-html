How to set fixed price for print area?

To set a fixed price for rectangular print area,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Print Profile in the toolbar towards the left.
  3. Select your print profile and click on the edit icon.
  4. In the new page, go to Price Sheet.
  5. A Price Table will appear on the right. Choose Area Price from the first drop-down.
  6. Enter your price for print area.
  7. Click Save.
Your fixed price for print area (rectangular) has been saved.

Example:
(i) Product having Print Area as A4 and the quantity range is 15 then $6.65 will be added to the total printing cost.
Print Area is A4
Quantity Range is 20
Print price will be $6.65

The price will be shown to the customer is :

Product Price + Print price = Total price ($15 + $6.65 = $21.65)

(ii) Product having Print Area as A5 and the quantity range is 50 then $4.60 will be added to the total printing cost.
Print Area is A5
Quantity Range is 50
Print price will be $4.60

The price will be shown to the customer is :

Product Price + Print price = Total price ($15 + $4.60 = $19.60)
