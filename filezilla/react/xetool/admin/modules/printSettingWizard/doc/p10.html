How to enable background price?

NOTE: You can set price for premium backgrounds. Its price will be added to the total printing cost when your customers use backgrounds from your premium collection.

To enable background price,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Print Profile in the toolbar towards the left.
  3. Select your print profile and click on the edit icon.
  4. In the new page, go to Price Settings.
  5. Under price settings, find Enable Background Price and enable it.
  6. Click Save.
You have enabled background price.

NOTE: You need to set the price of all your premium backgrounds beforehand.

Example:
A Background price is set as $1.50. When your customer use the same background for the customization for the product, the price set for that background will be added to the total printing cost.
So the price will be as :

Product Price + Background Price = Total Price ($25 + $1.50 = $26.50)
