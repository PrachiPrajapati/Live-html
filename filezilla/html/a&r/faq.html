FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

01 How many songs can I upload?

There is no maximum, but leave companies wanting more. Adding too much too soon leaves nothing to look forward to. So allow the A&R's the opportunity to ask for more. You can change your content throughout the duration of your membership.

02Do I have to pay Talent Finders a fee should I be signed from the site?

No, we only provide the means for you to be discovered. Once you're signed, that's it, we wish you all the best at this time.

03Do you offer any legal support?

I'm afraid not, not at this time.?

04What if the record company I want to get the attention of is not linked to the site?

You can invite them, just send them an email link to your profile and we will allow them access to view your profile.

05Can anyone view my profile and listen to my music?

No, only record companies and the administrators of the site. Anyone else will need a direct invite from you.

06What can I do to get my profile in the top 10?

Be The best you can be. The top 10 profiles are selected by the professionals on the Talent Finders team who listen and select according to what they hear.

07What about copyright. How am I protected on your site?

The copyright of your music & pictures is entirely down to you. We only provide privacy on our site only.
