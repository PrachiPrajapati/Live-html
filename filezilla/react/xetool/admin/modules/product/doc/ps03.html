Shape Printing Area

How to add print area: any shape ?

NOTE: Supported format is svg.
To add print area for any shape,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Products in the toolbar towards the left and select Product List from the drop down.
  3. The product list will appear towards the right. Select your product.
  4. Click on Shape Printing Area from the drop-down section of Decoration area type towards the right.
  5. Click on Create Custom Mask located under Select decoration area.
  6. A popup named Configure Custom Mask will open. Click Add Mask.
  7. Set properties such as, upload SVG mask, mask name, height, width, etc., and click on Save icon.
  8. Close the popup.
You have added print area for any shape.

How to configure print area: irregular shape ?

To configure variable printing area for irregular shapes,
TIP: Supported format is svg.
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Products in the toolbar towards the left and select Product List from the drop down.
  3. The product list will appear towards the right. Select your product.
  4. Click on Shape Printing Area from the drop-down section of Decoration area type towards the right.
  5. Click Create Custom Mask located under Select decoration area.
  6. A popup named Configure Custom Mask will open. Click Add Mask.
  7. A popup will open. Go to your desired shape and make the changes.
  8. Close the pop-up.
You can view the newly configured mask from the dropdown section of Select decoration area.

How to modify print area: irregular shape ?

To modify a product print area (irregular shape),
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Products in the toolbar towards the left and select Product List from the drop down.
  3. The product list will appear towards the right. Select your product.
  4. Click on Shape Printing Areafrom the drop-down section of Decoration area type towards the right.
  5. Click Create Custom Mask located under Select decoration area.
  6. A pop up will open. Update the properties of the desired shape, such as mask name, width, height and price.
  7. Once done, click Update to save the changes.
  8. Close the pop-up.
    9. You have modified print area for irregular shapes.

How to delete print area: irregular shape ?

To delete print area: irregular shape,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Products in the toolbar towards the left and select Product List from the drop down.
  3. The product list will appear towards the right. Select your product.
  4. Click on Shape Printing Area from the drop-down section of Decoration area type towards the right.
  5. Click Create Custom Mask located under Select decoration area.
  6. A pop-up named Configure Custom Mask will open. Go to your selected mask and click on delete icon.
  7. Another pop-up will open asking you to confirm your action. Click Continue.
  8. Close the pop-up.
Your print area for irregular shapes has been deleted.
