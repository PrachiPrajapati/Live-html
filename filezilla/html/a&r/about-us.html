OUR STORY

A&R TALENT FINDERS WAS CREATED IN 2017 BY PROFESSIONAL MUSICIAN/SINGER AND SONGWRITER, KEITH ALEXANDA.

Witnessing the growth of talent entering into the music industry, alongside the popularity of TV talent shows and online music platforms, Keith saw a need for talented people to have a forum that would help them to showcase their skills without compromise and with a Direct link to creative developers and supporters.

Creating A&R TalentFinders felt like an obvious and exciting venture. The idea was original created in 2006 and over the years the idea kept growing and was eventually put into development in late 2014.

Several designers and developers were recruited along the way to bring his vision to life, and along with the approval and acceptance of some the of the most influential record companies in the industry that welcomed the arrival of this innovative website, music talent from all around can now showcase their talent to an approved audience of A&R Executives.

Passion, hard work and reverence for the art of music has brought this website into existence, and we at A&R Talent Finders hope those venturing into a career as a music practitioner take full advantage of our service to achieve their goals of music industry success!.
