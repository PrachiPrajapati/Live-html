During the Covid19 pandemic, it is important that we stay at home, protect the NHS and save lives. Government advice is that you should stay at home, and only go outside for food, collect medicines, or work if you can’t work at home. Whilst most people can still visit a pharmacy, some cannot leave their home and will require their medication to be delivered to them.

We will deliver your medication to your house if you are in any of the following groups:

Very high risk

and must be shielded for 12 weeks

Over 60

and choosing to self isolate

High risk

e.g. asthma, diabetes and choosing to self isolate

Have Covid19 symptoms

fever, dry cough, shortness of breath

Unable to get to a pharmacy

to collect your prescription

Haxby Group Pharmacy Limited is a York owned group of 7 pharmacies that cover the whole of the city of York. By supporting us, you’re supporting 60 local jobs.

We have a network of community volunteers in all zones and 6 delivery drivers that are able to cover all addresses in York. To register for your medication to be delivered, please complete the form below

Registration Form

Once you have completed the form, we will telephone you to explain how to order your prescription and when your medication will be delivered.

We will store your data securely to contact you regarding our delivery service. Your data will not be used for any other purpose and is never shared with 3rd parties and will not be kept longer than necessary.
