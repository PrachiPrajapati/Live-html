How to set price for size variants?

INFO: The cost of printing on a bigger size product (XXL, XXXL, 4XL, 5XL) will be certainly higher as compared to printing on smaller sizes (S, M, L). However, companies tend to ignore this and keep the product price same for all sizes, which might incur losses. This feature allows you to compensate for that loss.
To set price for size variants of a product,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Products in the toolbar towards the left and select Product List from the drop down.
  3. The product list will appear towards the right. Select your product.
  4. In the product page, go to Price Rules.
  5. From the three options towards the left, select Size Variants.
  6. The size list will populate towards the right. Enter the value below respective print methods (DTG, Screen Print, etc.) on the row of your desired size variant.
  7. Once done, scroll down and click Save.
Your price for size variant has been saved.
