How to enable scaling price?

INFO: Scaling images or text consumes more ink, thereby pushing your printing costs higher. By enabling scaling price, you can bill your customers extra if their designs exceed the standard size.

To enable scaling price,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Print Profile in the toolbar towards the left.
  3. Select your print profile and click on the edit icon.
  4. In the new page, go to Price Settings.
  5. Under price settings, find Enable Scaling Price and enable it.
  6. Give desired scaling price in the box that appears below.
  7. Click Save.
You have enabled scaling price.
Example:
If you have set scaling price at $1 per square unit, your customers will be charged $1 extra for every square unit as they scale the image size.
So price will be showing as :

Product Price + Print Price = Total Price ($20 + $5.25 = $25.25)
