How to enable image quality rating?

Note: It is meant for a preview of the quality of the image compared to the actual print size. The star rating changes on scaling of the image.

  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to the toolbar section to the left, then click Settings
  3. In the Settings page, click Image Quality Rating.
  4. Click Enable and then click Save
You've enabled image quality rating.

Note: If you reduce size of the image, the quality of print would increase; on the other hand increasing the size, the quality of print would decrease.
