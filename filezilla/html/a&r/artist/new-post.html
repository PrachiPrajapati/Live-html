TERMS OF USE

This is a free service for the existing members and companies affiliated to the A&R Talent Finders website which allows you to post information about....

  • Upcoming events ie. Showcases
  • Instruments for sale
  • Instruments wanted
  • Musicians wanted
  • Studio for hire
  • Rehearsal Space
  • Auditions
  • We will also allow you to connect with other musicians by posting ads stating what you need.

Notices for anything outside of the listed accepted criteria will be removed. We DO NOT allow you to promote your own business via this service and any ad shown to be doing this will be removed. Continuous abuse of our service will result in your account being removed permanently.

Be Safe:
  • Please do not send money to any ad.
  • Make sure you have an address and inform someone where you are going.
  • If meeting in a public place try to take a companion.
  • Travel with someone where possible.
  • If at all unsure about the legitimacy of any of the adds, please inform ARTF.
  • ARTF cannot and will not accept responsibility for any unscrupulous ads but will do our best to reject and delete them once discovered.
