How to set default designs category?

  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to the toolbar section to the left, then click Graphics.
  3. From the drop-down menu select Designs.
  4. Go to the top right corner and click on All Categories > Subcategory.
  5. A pop-up will open. Select the category and subcategory from the drop-down menu.
  6. Click Save.
You have now set default designs category.

Note: Tool will load with the default categories and subcategories as set in the admin panel.

How to manage clipart category?

To manage (add/edit/delete) clipart category,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Graphics in the toolbar towards the left and select Designs.
  3. Move to the top-right-corner and tap Manage Categories.
  4. A box will appear. You can take following actions there:
    • Add a category - In the left column under Design Categories, simply enter a name and click Add.
    • Edit a category - Hover the mouse over your selected category and click on edit icon.
    • Delete a category - Hover the mouse over your selected category and click on delete icon.
  5. Once done, tap outside the box to close it.

Your changes have been successfully applied.

How to manage clipart subcategory?

To manage (add/edit/delete) clipart subcategory,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Graphics in the toolbar towards the left and select Designs.
  3. Move to the top-right-corner and tap Manage Categories.
  4. A box will appear. You can take following actions there:
    • Add a subcategory - Select your category and move right towards the box under Design Subcategories. Simply, enter your subcategory name there and click Add.
    • Edit a subcategory - Select your category and move right to see the existing subcategories. Then, hover the mouse over your selected subcategory and click on edit icon.
    • Delete a subcategory - Select your category and move right to see the existing subcategories. Then, hover the mouse over your selected subcategory and click on delete icon.
  5. Once done, tap outside the box to close it.

Your changes have been successfully applied.
