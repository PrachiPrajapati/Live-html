How to manage color palette category?

To manage color palette category,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Color Palettes in the toolbar towards the left.
  3. Move to the top-right-corner and tap Manage Categories.
  4. A box will appear. You can take following actions there:
    • Add a category - Under Palette Categories, simply enter a name and click Add.
    • Edit a category - Hover the mouse over your selected color palette category and click on edit icon.
    • Delete a category - Hover the mouse over your selected color palette category and click on delete icon.
  5. Once done, tap outside the box to close it.

Your changes have been successfully applied.
