Track your health

CarePack - your most important health information always and everywhere
under control

Care Pack is the new system that centrally stores data about your health and you can even manage individually.
  • Emergency function with GPS submitting your current position and your heart rate with blood group.
  • Heart rate with history up to 7 days.
  • Body Mass Index.
  • Medical findings.
  • An overview of your medicines.
  • Vaccination certificate overview with automatic memory function.
