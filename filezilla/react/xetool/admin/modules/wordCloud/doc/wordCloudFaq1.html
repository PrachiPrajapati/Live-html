How to add word cloud?

To add word cloud,

NOTE: Supported formats are png, jpg, jpeg and bmp.

TIP: You can also add word clouds in bulk. Simply select multiple word clouds and follow same instructions.

  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Text in the toolbar towards the left and select Word Cloud.
  3. Move to the right and tap Add New Word Cloud.
  4. A box will appear. Click Upload Word Cloud to upload a word cloud and set its property, such as name.
  5. Click Save.
Your word cloud has been added.

How to edit word cloud?

To edit word cloud,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Text in the toolbar towards the left and select Word Cloud.
  3. Move to the right and you will see the available word clouds.
  4. Now, select the word cloud you want to edit and click Edit Selected.
  5. A new page will appear where you can edit property of selected word cloud, such as its name.
  6. Make your changes and click Save.
TIP: You can also edit word clouds in bulk. Simply select multiple word clouds and follow same instructions.

Your word cloud has been edited.

How to delete word cloud?

To delete word cloud,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Text in the toolbar towards the left and select Word Cloud.
  3. Move to the right and you will see the available word clouds.
  4. Now, select the word cloud you want to delete and click Delete Selected.
  5. A pop-up will open asking you to confirm your action.
  6. Click Confirm.
TIP: You can also delete word clouds in bulk. Simply select multiple word clouds and follow same instructions.

Your word cloud has been deleted.
