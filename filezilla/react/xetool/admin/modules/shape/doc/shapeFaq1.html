How to add shape?

To add shape,

NOTE: Supported format is svg only.

TIP: You can also add shapes in bulk. Simply select multiple shapes and follow same instructions.

  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Graphics in the toolbar towards the left and select Shapes.
  3. Move to the right and tap Add New Shape.
  4. A box will appear. Click Upload Shape to upload a shape and set its properties, such as name, tags, and category.
  5. Click Save.
Your shape has been added.

How to edit shape?

To edit shape,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Graphics in the toolbar towards the left and select Shapes.
  3. Move to the right and you will see the available shapes.
  4. Now, select the shape you want to edit and click Edit Selected.
  5. A box will appear where you can edit properties of selected shape, such as name, tags, and category.
  6. Make your changes and click Save.
TIP: You can also edit shapes in bulk. Simply select multiple shapes and follow same instructions.

Your shape has been edited.

How to delete shape?

To delete shape,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Graphics in the toolbar towards the left and select Shapes.
  3. Move to the right and you will see the available shapes.
  4. Now, select the shape you want to delete and click Delete Selected.
  5. A pop-up will open asking you to confirm your action.
  6. Click Confirm.
TIP: You can also delete shapes in bulk. Simply select multiple shapes and follow same instructions.

Your shape has been deleted.
