How to set fixed price for irregular print area?

To set fixed price for irregular print area,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Print Profile in the toolbar towards the left.
  3. Select your print profile and click on the edit icon.
  4. In the new page, go to Price Sheet.
  5. A Price Table will appear on the right. Choose Area Price from the first drop-down.
  6. Now, scroll down to find Mask Area Price and click Configure Mask Price.
  7. A popup will open. Enter your price for irregular shape and save it. Close the popup.
  8. Click Save or Save & Continue.
Your fixed price for irregular print area has been saved.
