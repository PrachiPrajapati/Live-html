How to enable whitebase price ?

To enable whitebase price,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Products in the toolbar towards the left and select the Product List from the drop down.
  3. The product list will appear towards the right. Select your product.
  4. On the product page, go to Price Rules.
  5. From the three options towards the left, select Whitebase.
  6. The color variant list will populate towards the right. Select checkboxes of the color variants under respective print methods (DTG, Screen Print, etc.) for which you would like to enable whitebase.
  7. Click Save.
You have enabled whitebase.
NOTE: After enabling whitebase for a product, you need to set price for whitebase in the price sheet.
