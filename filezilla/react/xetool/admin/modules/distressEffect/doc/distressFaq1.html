How to add distress effect?

To add distress effects,

NOTE: Supported formats are jpg & png.

TIP: You can also add distress effects in bulk. Simply select multiple distress effect images and upload.

  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Graphics in the toolbar towards the left and select Distress Effect.
  3. Move to the right and tap Add Distress.
  4. A box will appear. Click Upload Distress Image. Set property of the distress effect image you upload, such as name.
  5. Click Save.
Your distress effect has been added.

How to edit distress effect?

To edit distress effect,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Graphics in the toolbar towards the left and select Distress Effect.
  3. Move to the right and you will see the available distress effects.
  4. Now, select the distress effect you want to edit and click Edit Selected.
  5. A box will appear where you can edit property of selected distress effect, such as name.
  6. Make your changes and click Save.

Your distress effect has been edited.

How to delete distress effect?

To delete distress effect,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Graphics in the toolbar towards the left and select Distress Effects.
  3. Move to the right and you will see the available distress effects.
  4. Now, select the distress effect you want to delete and click Delete Selected.
  5. A pop-up will open asking you to confirm your action.
  6. Click Confirm.
TIP: You can also delete distress effects in bulk. Simply select multiple distress effects and follow same instructions.

Your distress effect has been deleted.
