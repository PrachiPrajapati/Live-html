How to assign products to print profile?

To assign products to print profile,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Print Profile in the toolbar towards the left.
  3. Select your print profile and click on the edit icon.
  4. In the new page, go to Assign Assets.
  5. Under "assign to the following", go to Products.
  6. Select checkboxes of the items inside that you want to assign as categories.
  7. Scroll down and click Save or Save & Continue.
Note: Please select at least one category from each asset to save successfully.

You have assigned products to print profile.
