How to enable direct cart posting ?

NOTE: Enabling direct cart posting will add the customized product to cart directly without showing any intermediate pop-up.
To enable direct cart posting,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Settings in the toolbar towards the left.
  3. Move right to locate and tap Direct Cart Post.
  4. Inside, there are two options. Click Enable.
  5. Click Save.
You have enabled direct cart posting.
