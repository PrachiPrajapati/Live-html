Rectangle Printing Area

How to add print area: rectangular shape ?

To add rectangular print area,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Products in the toolbar towards the left and select Product List from the drop down.
  3. The product list will appear towards the right. Select your product.
  4. Click on Rectangle Printing Area from the drop-down section of Decoration area type towards the right.
  5. Then click Create Custom Decoration Area located under Select Decoration Area.
  6. A popup named Configure Custom Print Area will open. Click Add New Size .
  7. Set properties such as, size name, width and height and click on save icon.
  8. Close the popup.
You have added rectangular print area.

How to modify print area ?

To modify rectangular print area,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Products in the toolbar towards the left and select Product List from the drop down.
  3. The product list will appear towards the right. Select your product.
  4. Click on Rectangle Printing Area from the drop-down section of Decoration area type towards the right.
  5. Click on Create Custom Decoration Area located under Select Decoration Area.
  6. A popup named Configure Custom Size will open. Go to your desired size and modify properties, such as name, height, and width.
  7. Once done, click Update to save your changes.
  8. Close the popup.
You have modified rectangular print area.

How to delete print area: rectangular shape?

To delete print area for rectangular shapes,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Products in the toolbar towards the left and select Product List from the drop down.
  3. The product list will appear towards the right. Select your product.
  4. Click on Rectangle Printing Area from the drop-down section of Decoration area type towards the right.
  5. Click on Create Custom Decoration Area located under Select Decoration Area Type.
  6. A popup named Configure Custom Print Area will open. Go to your selected size and click on delete icon.
  7. Then click Update.
  8. Close the popup.
Your print area for rectangular shapes has been deleted.
