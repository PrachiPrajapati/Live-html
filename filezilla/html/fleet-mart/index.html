Home Screen

First sign in/ sign up Fleet Mart, After you sign in, you will be directed to the Home Screen dashboard. Advanced Search

Fleet Mart app is equipped with extensive advanced search filters to help buyers find exactly what they are looking for. The Advanced Search option allows you to filter Commercial Vehicle and equipment using variables such as category, sub category, manufacturer, Variant/model, Purchase year, price and location. This helps you to find your desired Commercial vehicle and equipment while cutting down on your browsing time.

Call Request

Initially, buyers can contact sellers through Chat and further raise a Call Request. Contact details of sellers are shared only when the Call Request is accepted. As a seller, this saves you the panic of having to attend unwanted calls, as the power to choose with whom you want to interact is in your hands.

Solving a Problem, changing the dynamics

The Pre-owned Commercial Vehicles, heavy equipment and Machineries market In India is unorganized and fragmented which makes it difficult to find the right buyer at the right time and within the right cost. Also, it's difficult to find the right seller with the right Vehicle and equipment at the right time and within the right cost. Unsold Commercial Vehicles and heavy equipment block seller's capital and space, also face some issues like undue depreciation, as well as unwanted maintenance and storage costs. Whereas delay in finding the right Commercial Vehicles and equipment on the buyer's end results in loss of productivity. There is always the lingering anxiety about genuineness of the buyer or seller.

Fleet Mart is a radical solution to this problem where genuine buyers and sellers come together in an organized e-marketplace, thus reducing time and cost as well as eliminating anxiety involved in the process.
