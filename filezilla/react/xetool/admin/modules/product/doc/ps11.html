How to assign template to product?


TIP: Make sure you have created a few product templates before proceeding.
To assign template to product,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Products in the toolbar towards the left and select Product List from the drop down.
  3. The product list will appear towards the right. Select your product.
  4. In the product page, tap Settings..
  5. Enable Product Skeleton Image and click on the drop-down.
  6. Select your desired template.
  7. Click Save.
Your template has been assigned to product.
