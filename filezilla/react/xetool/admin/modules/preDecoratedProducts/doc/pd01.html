Pre-decorated Product Template

How to edit pre-decorated product ?

  1. Open inkXE Admin Panel.
  2. Go to the toolbar section to the left, then click Products.
  3. From the drop-down menu select Pre-Decorated Products.
  4. A new page will appear where you hover the mouse to choose from a list of your pre-decorated products that you want to edit.
  5. A designer tool pop-up screen will appear where you can edit your previous designed template.
  6. Once done, click Save Pre-Decorated product.
  7. A pop-up of Lock properties will appear where you can check/uncheck the lock feature of each design element and click Save.
Finally, you have successfully edited the pre-decorated product.

How to add pre-decorated product template as product ?

  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Products in the toolbar towards the left and select Pre-Decorated products from the drop-down..
  3. A new page will appear where you hover the mouse to choose from a list of your pre-decorated products that you can add it as a product in your Ecommerce store.
  4. Click Add As Product.
  5. A new page will appear. Set properties of selected pre-decoretd product such as name, sku, description, color, etc.
  6. Click Save.
You have now deleted Pre-Decorated Products.
NOTE: Once your product is saved, you will be asked if you would like to add another color and size. If yes, click on Add another color variant.

How to delete pre-decorated products ?

  1. Open inkXE Admin Panel.
  2. Go to the toolbar section to the left, then click Products.
  3. From the drop-down menu select Pre-Decorated Products.
  4. The pre-decorated product list will appear towards the right. Hover on the pre-decorated product which you want to delete and click Delete.
  5. A pop-up will open asking you to confirm your action.
  6. Click Confirm.
You have now deleted Pre-Decorated Products.
