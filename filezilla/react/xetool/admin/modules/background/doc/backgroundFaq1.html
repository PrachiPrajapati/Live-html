How to add designs background?

To add background,

NOTE: Supported formats are png, jpeg, jpg & bmp.

TIP: You can also add background in bulk. Simply select Background Image and upload multiple images.

  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Graphics in the toolbar towards the left and select Designs Background.
  3. Move to the right and tap Add Background.
  4. A box will appear. Here, you can take any one of the following actions:
    • Click Background Color and select a color from the color picker.
    • Click Background Image and upload a background.
    Set properties of the design you upload, such as name, price, tags, and category.
  5. Click Save.
Your designs background has been added.

How to edit designs background?

To edit designs background,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Graphics in the toolbar towards the left and select Designs Background.
  3. Move to the right and you will see the available designs backgrounds.
  4. Now, select the designs background you want to edit and click Edit Selected.
  5. A box will appear where you can edit properties of selected designs background, such as name, price, and category.
  6. Make your changes and click Save.
TIP: You can also edit designs backgrounds in bulk. Simply select multiple designs backgrounds and follow same instructions.

Your designs background has been edited.

How to delete designs background?

To delete designs background,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Graphics in the toolbar towards the left and select Designs Background.
  3. Move to the right and you will see the available designs backgrounds.
  4. Now, select the designs background you want to delete and click Delete Selected.
  5. A pop-up will open asking you to confirm your action.
  6. Click Confirm.
TIP: You can also delete designs backgrounds in bulk. Simply select multiple designs backgrounds and follow same instructions.

Your designs background has been deleted.
