How to enable additional price?

INFO: When it comes to printing, there are many types of costs included and it is likely that we might skip something. This is where Additional price comes in. Set an amount here for expenses that were otherwise not covered in the rest of the options.

To set additional price,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Print Profile in the toolbar towards the left.
  3. Select your print profile and click on the edit icon.
  4. In the new page, go to Price Settings.
  5. Under price settings, find Additional Price and enable it.
  6. Give desired additional price in the box that appears below.
  7. Set a price and click Save.
You have enabled additional price.

Example:
Additional Price is set as $2.50 in inkXE Admin. When your customer customize the product then extra $2.50 will be added to the total printing cost.
So the price will be showing to the customer as :

Product Price + Additional Price = Total price ($15 + $2.50 = $17.50)
