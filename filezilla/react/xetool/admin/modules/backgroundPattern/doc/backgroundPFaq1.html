How to add background pattern?

To add background pattern,

NOTE: Supported formats are png, jpeg, jpg & bmp.
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Graphics in the toolbar towards the left and select Background Pattern.
  3. Move to the right and tap Add Pattern.
  4. A box will appear. Click Add Pattern. Set properties of the background pattern you upload, such as name, price and category.
  5. Click Save.
Your background pattern has been added.

How to edit background pattern?

To edit background pattern,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Graphics in the toolbar towards the left and select Background Pattern.
  3. Move to the right and you will see the available background patterns.
  4. Now, select the background patterns you want to edit and click Edit Selected.
  5. A box will appear where you can edit properties of selected background pattern, such as name, price, and category.
  6. Make your changes and click Save.
TIP: You can also edit background patterns in bulk. Simply select multiple background patterns and follow same instructions.

Your background pattern has been edited.

How to delete background pattern?

To delete background pattern,
  1. Open inkXE Admin.
  2. Go to Graphics in the toolbar towards the left and select Background Pattern.
  3. Move to the right and you will see the available background patterns.
  4. Now, select the background patterns you want to delete and click Delete Selected.
  5. A pop-up will open asking you to confirm your action.
  6. Click Confirm.
TIP: You can also delete background patterns in bulk. Simply select multiple background patterns and follow same instructions.

Your background pattern has been deleted.
